Research Projects

  • "Serial Killers and the Dark Triad" - Master's Thesis - Northeastern State University - May 2023
    • Investigated the relationship between Dark Triad personality traits (narcissism, Machiavellianism, psychopathy) and serial killer behavior through a comprehensive literature review and analysis of case studies.
    • Found significant correlations between high levels of Dark Triad traits and the likelihood of engaging in serial killing behavior, contributing to the understanding of criminal psychology.
  • "The Impact of Childhood Trauma on Adult Attachment Styles" - Undergraduate Research Project - Northeastern State University - December 2020
    • Explored how various forms of childhood trauma influence adult attachment styles using surveys and psychological assessments.
    • Results indicated that individuals with a history of childhood trauma were more likely to develop insecure attachment styles, highlighting the long-term effects of early adverse experiences.
